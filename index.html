Our Founder

Tej

Founder & CEO

Tej is the Founder & CEO of Potentia Nexus Ltd, creator of Aadhya, a privacy-first AI platform for individuals with ADHD. With a Master's in Business Analytics and experience as a Data Analyst at Orderly and Halfords, Tej specializes in using data to tackle challenges in mental health and productivity. His goal is to build impactful solutions that improve well-being and simplify life for those facing cognitive challenges.
